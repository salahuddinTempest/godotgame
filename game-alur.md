# Game Alur — Royal Era: Kingdom Chronicles

**Story Setting**: Medieval Kingdom Era, Year 847 of the Royal Calendar  
**Main Theme**: Rise from humble adventurer to legendary hero through co-op adventures

---

## BAGIAN 1: 20 ALUR UTAMA GAME (Main Storyline Arcs)

### Arc 1: THE AWAKENING (Level 1-2)
**Lokasi Utama**: Riverside Village → Peaceful Forest Outskirts

**Narasi**:
Pemain adalah sekelompok petualang baru yang terdampar di desa kecil bernama Riverside. Desa ini digerebek oleh kelompok bandits. Penduduk meminta bantuan para pemain untuk melakukan odd jobs untuk membuktikan kompetensi mereka.

**Key Events**:
1. Tiba di Riverside Village
2. Meet dengan Village Elder
3. Terima job pertama: Clear rats di gudang desa
4. Bandit scouts spotted di dekat desa

**Boss**: None (intro quest)  
**Reward**: 200 Gold, Basic Equipment, 500 XP  
**Unlock**: Side quests di Riverside

---

### Arc 2: THE BANDIT PROBLEM (Level 2-3)
**Lokasi Utama**: Peaceful Forest, Bandits Camp

**Narasi**:
Bandits dipimpin **Captain Blackthorn** terus mengganggu desa. Village Elder minta para pemain menginvestigasi dan menangani masalah bandits ini sebelum mereka menyerang langsung.

**Key Events**:
1. Scout bandit camp
2. Gather intelligence tentang Blackthorn
3. Confront bandit scouts
4. Decide: Direct assault atau sneaky approach

**Boss**: Bandit Captain (Level 4, 200 HP)  
**Reward**: 400 Gold, Leather Armor, 800 XP  
**Choice Impact**: Affects village relations

---

### Arc 3: KINGDOM'S FIRST CALL (Level 3-5)
**Lokasi Utama**: Riverside → Kingdom Capital (Goldstone City) - Travel sequence

**Narasi**:
Setelah menangani bandits lokal, messenger dari Kingdom Capital tiba dengan summons. Diketahui ada ancaman lebih besar di Shadowveil Dungeon yang mulai aktif lagi. Para pemain diminta untuk bergabung dengan Royal Guard.

**Key Events**:
1. Receive official letter dari Kingdom
2. Journey ke Goldstone City (travel hazards)
3. Meet dengan Lord Chancellor
4. Receive official commission sebagai Kingdom Adventurers
5. Learn tentang Shadowveil history

**Boss**: Bandits ambush during travel (Level 4, 6 enemies)  
**Reward**: 500 Gold, Royal Commission Badge, 1000 XP  
**Unlock**: Kingdom Guard quests, merchant discount

---

### Arc 4: DUNGEON PREPARATION (Level 5-7)
**Lokasi Utama**: Goldstone City - Multiple locations

**Narasi**:
Sebelum enter Shadowveil Dungeon yang dangerous, para pemain harus prepare dengan ngumpulin information, supplies, dan training. Ada hints tentang apa yang akan mereka hadapi di dalam.

**Key Events**:
1. Visit Royal Archives untuk research dungeon
2. Meet dengan experts: Scholars, enchanters, priests
3. Collect enchantments dan special items
4. Train di Royal Guard barracks
5. Scout dungeon entrance

**Boss**: Training bout dengan Royal Guard Captain (Level 6, sparring)  
**Reward**: Enchanted items, knowledge books, 1200 XP  
**Unlock**: Shadowveil Dungeon access

---

### Arc 5: SHADOW RISING - LEVEL 1 (Level 7-10)
**Lokasi Utama**: Shadowveil Dungeon Level 1

**Narasi**:
Para pemain finally enter Shadowveil Dungeon. Level 1 is ancient temple area filled with corrupted creatures. Mereka discover ancient murals tentang ritual yang dimulai. Reality mulai twist dengan dark magic.

**Key Events**:
1. Enter dungeon, encounter first corrupted creatures
2. Find ancient murals (exposition)
3. Discover ritual chamber (incomplete)
4. Meet mysterious hooded figure (mysterious ally)
5. Escape before cave collapse

**Boss**: Corrupted Guardian (Level 9, 400 HP)  
**Reward**: 800 Gold, Shadow Ward Amulet, 1500 XP  
**Plot Point**: First hint about Shards of Darkness

---

### Arc 6: THE CULT EMERGES (Level 10-12)
**Lokasi Utama**: Dungeon exit → Kingdom Capital → Dark Forest

**Narasi**:
Dengan dungeon mulai menunjukkan activity, intelligence reveals ada organized cult yang bekerja dari dalam kingdom untuk membuka dungeon ini. Para pemain harus identify cult members dan prevent ritual.

**Key Events**:
1. Return ke capital dengan findings
2. Investigate suspicious merchants
3. Stake out cult meeting
4. Identify first cult members
5. Arrest attempts (some escape)

**Boss**: Cult Wizard (Level 11, 500 HP)  
**Reward**: 1000 Gold, Cult documents, 1800 XP  
**Unlock**: Cult investigation quests

---

### Arc 7: MOUNTAIN PASS EXPEDITION (Level 12-15)
**Lokasi Utama**: Mountain Pass, Northern Route, Trading posts

**Narasi**:
Intel suggests cult materials coming dari north. Para pemain perlu lead expedition ke mountain pass untuk intercept supplies dan gather information tentang northern connections. Journey is dangerous dengan environmental hazards.

**Key Events**:
1. Prepare expedition supplies
2. Travel through mountain pass
3. Encounter bandits dan monsters
4. Find cult supply caravan
5. Discover route leads ke north

**Boss**: Cult Caravan Master (Level 14, 600 HP) + 4 guards  
**Reward**: 1200 Gold, valuable materials, 2000 XP  
**Unlock**: Northern region access

---

### Arc 8: DARK FOREST CONSPIRACY (Level 15-18)
**Lokasi Utama**: Dark Forest, Abandoned Monastery, Ancient ruins

**Narasi**:
Deep dalam Dark Forest, ada Abandoned Monastery yang served sebagai cult headquarters sebelumnya. Para pemain discover bahwa cult organization lebih kompleks dari yang dipikirkan, dengan network yang extensive dan powerful members.

**Key Events**:
1. Scout dark forest
2. Locate abandoned monastery
3. Find cult archives (research area)
4. Discover higher-level cult members
5. Ambush attempt oleh cult response team

**Boss**: Cult High Priest (Level 17, 700 HP) + 2 ritualists  
**Reward**: 1500 Gold, High Priest's robes, 2500 XP  
**Plot Twist**: Discover cult leader identity

---

### Arc 9: SHADOW RISING - LEVEL 2 (Level 18-22)
**Lokasi Utama**: Shadowveil Dungeon Level 2

**Narasi**:
Dengan informasi baru tentang cult plans, para pemain return ke Shadowveil untuk go deeper. Level 2 is underground city area dengan more powerful corrupted creatures. Ritual progress terlihat lebih advanced.

**Key Events**:
1. Re-enter dungeon dengan new knowledge
2. Explore level 2 underground city
3. Discover ritual progress chamber
4. Find prophecy stones
5. Encounter stronger corrupted beings

**Boss**: Ancient Guardian (Level 20, 900 HP) + corrupted minions  
**Reward**: 1800 Gold, prophecy fragments, 3000 XP  
**Plot Point**: Prophecy reveals 7 cycles before full awakening

---

### Arc 10: THE BROKEN KINGDOM (Level 22-26)
**Lokasi Utama**: Goldstone City, Royal Castle, Multiple ministry districts

**Narasi**:
Reveal bahwa multiple high-ranking officials adalah cult members. Kingdom enters chaos dengan paranoia. Para pemain harus navigate political intrigue sambil continuing pursue cult. Kingdom declares martial law.

**Key Events**:
1. Discover official conspirators
2. Political meetings dengan loyal nobles
3. Race untuk secure evidence
4. Confront corrupt officials (some fight, some flee)
5. Kingdom reorganizes defense

**Boss**: Duke of Eastern Province (Level 24, 850 HP) + guards  
**Reward**: 2000 Gold, Royal Warrant, 3500 XP  
**Impact**: Major world state change

---

### Arc 11: ALLIANCE FORGED (Level 26-30)
**Lokasi Utama**: Neutral Territory, Diplomatic locations, Trading hubs

**Narasi**:
Dengan kingdom weakened, para pemain must forge alliances dengan surrounding territories dan factions. Ini critical untuk facing upcoming threats. Political machinations become important as combat.

**Key Events**:
1. Meet dengan various faction leaders
2. Complete diplomatic quests
3. Negotiate alliances
4. Establish supply networks
5. Prepare coalition forces

**Boss**: Rival adventurer team (Level 28, 5v5 battle)  
**Reward**: 2200 Gold, alliance tokens, 4000 XP  
**Unlock**: Faction-specific quests

---

### Arc 12: THE NORTHERN THREAT (Level 30-34)
**Lokasi Utama**: Northmarch Province, Snow Wastes, Mountain fortress

**Narasi**:
Cult activity traced ke north. Para pemain discover bahwa **Frost King**, ancient dragon awakening, has made pact dengan cult untuk resurrect. Northern clans caught dalam conflict antara cult dan dragon influence.

**Key Events**:
1. Travel ke northern territories
2. Meet dengan northern clan leaders
3. Learn tentang dragon prophecy
4. Infiltrate cult outpost di north
5. Witness ritual progress

**Boss**: Malachar's Northern Lieutenant (Level 32, 1000 HP)  
**Reward**: 2500 Gold, Frost artifacts, 4500 XP  
**Unlock**: Dragon-related questline

---

### Arc 13: SUNKEN SECRETS (Level 34-38)
**Lokasi Utama**: Coastal regions, Underwater temple, Sunken ruins

**Narasi**:
Ancient tome reveals ada **Sunken Temple** containing second Shard of Darkness. Para pemain must dive into underwater caverns, solve ancient puzzles, dan confront guardians. Discover hints tentang ancient civilization dan true nature of Shards.

**Key Events**:
1. Reach coastal region
2. Learn underwater diving mechanics
3. Explore sunken temple
4. Decipher ancient writing
5. Find second Shard location

**Boss**: Deep Sea Guardian (Level 36, 1100 HP)  
**Reward**: 2800 Gold, ancient tome pages, 5000 XP  
**Unlock**: Underwater areas, water-based mechanics

---

### Arc 14: THE EASTERN ALLIANCE (Level 38-42)
**Lokasi Utama**: Enemy Kingdom Territory, Diplomatic chambers, Frontier

**Narasi**:
Para pemain must ally dengan **Enemy Kingdom** yang selama 100 tahun perang dengan home kingdom. Mereka juga menghadapi cult threat. Initial hostility harus overcome melalui diplomacy atau showing common enemy. Epic siege planning begins.

**Key Events**:
1. Travel ke enemy territory
2. Initial skirmishes (misunderstanding)
3. Diplomatic breakthrough
4. Learn tentang enemy kingdom's cult problem
5. Plan joint operation

**Boss**: Enemy Kingdom's Cult Commander (Level 40, 1200 HP)  
**Reward**: 3000 Gold, alliance armor, 5500 XP  
**Impact**: Major alliance shifts world politics

---

### Arc 15: SHADOW RISING - LEVEL 3 (Level 42-46)
**Lokasi Utama**: Shadowveil Dungeon Level 3 (deepest accessible level)

**Narasi**:
Para pemain venture deeper into Shadowveil Level 3, the abyssal depths. Ritual nearing completion. Mereka witness scale from ancient power yang mereka hadapi. Banyak visual hints tentang true threat's nature.

**Key Events**:
1. Navigate level 3's complex maze
2. Defeat powerful corrupted beings
3. Witness ritual chamber progress
4. Discover Shard holder identity
5. Escape before ritual completion

**Boss**: Ritual Master (Level 44, 1300 HP) + ritual slaves  
**Reward**: 3200 Gold, ritual knowledge, 6000 XP  
**Plot Point**: Shards almost complete, must act soon

---

### Arc 16: THE GREAT SIEGE (Level 46-50)
**Lokasi Utama**: Dark Fortress, Obsidian Tower approaches, Siege locations

**Narasi**:
Final preparation untuk ultimate assault pada Malachar's Dark Fortress. Para pemain lead coalition forces dalam massive siege warfare. Ini adalah culmination dari semua alliances dan preparations. Scale mencapai climax.

**Key Events**:
1. Gather coalition forces
2. Strategic siege planning
3. Assault fortress exterior
4. Breach inner defenses
5. Reach Obsidian Tower entrance

**Boss**: Fortress Commander (Level 48, 1500 HP) + siege bosses  
**Reward**: 3500 Gold, war hero medals, 6500 XP  
**Impact**: Major victory, path to final confrontation opens

---

### Arc 17: THE PROPHECY CHAMBER (Level 48-49)
**Lokasi Utama**: Ancient library in Obsidian Tower, Secret prophecy chamber

**Narasi**:
Before final battle, para pemain discover Secret Prophecy Chamber. Di sini, semua truth tentang Darkness, Shards, dan cosmic entity revealed. Mereka learn tentang sealing ritual yang diperlukan. Preparation untuk sacrifice diungkap.

**Key Events**:
1. Navigate tower library
2. Decode prophecies
3. Understand ritual mechanics
4. Meet dengan mysterious hooded figure again
5. Spiritual preparation untuk final battle

**Boss**: Prophecy Guardian (Level 49, 1400 HP)  
**Reward**: 3600 Gold, prophecy stone, 6800 XP  
**Plot Point**: Truth revealed, options for ending explained

---

### Arc 18: MALACHAR'S CHALLENGE (Level 49-50)
**Lokasi Utama**: Obsidian Tower, Throne chamber

**Narasi**:
Para pemain finally confront **High Priest Malachar** dalam his throne room. Dia sudah absorbed power dari Shards dan merasa nearly invincible. Epic duel follows dengan Malachar revealing true motivations dan history.

**Key Events**:
1. Dramatic entrance ke throne chamber
2. Confrontation dialogue
3. Malachar transforms
4. Phase 1: Magical combat
5. Phase 2: Corrupted form reveal

**Boss**: High Priest Malachar Phase 1 & 2 (Level 49-50, 1800 HP total)  
**Reward**: 4000 Gold, Malachar's grimoire, 7000 XP  
**Plot Point**: Malachar defeated but not sealed

---

### Arc 19: THE VOID AWAKENS (Level 50)
**Lokasi Utama**: Void Chamber (beyond Obsidian Tower)

**Narasi**:
Dengan Malachar defeated, ancient seals break. **Nyx, the Void Primordial** mulai manifest. Ini adalah cosmic horror, force beyond comprehension yang telah tried to consume existence sejak dawn of time. Final battle of the game.

**Key Events**:
1. Enter Void Chamber (reality breaking)
2. Nyx's first manifestation
3. Phase 1: Void attacks
4. Phase 2: Reality distortion
5. Phase 3: Final choice moment

**Boss**: Nyx, The Void Primordial (Level 50, 3000 HP + phases)  
**Reward**: 5000 Gold, choice-based unique rewards, 10000 XP  
**Impact**: Game ending determined

---

### Arc 20: THE ETERNAL SEAL / DIVERGENT ENDINGS (Level 50)
**Lokasi Utama**: Void Chamber, Multiple possible locations

**Narasi**:
Outcome depends pada choices throughout game. Ada multiple endings reflecting different decisions, sacrifices, dan alliance choices made. Tidak ada single "correct" ending.

**Ending Possibilities**:

**Ending A: Noble Sacrifice**
- One party member sealed entity permanently
- Heroes celebrated, world saved
- Sacrificed member becomes legend
- New Game+ with memorial quests

**Ending B: Shared Burden**
- All members share sealing power
- All gain buffs but also corrupted marks
- Pyrrhic victory, kingdom rebuilding
- Post-game content about redemption

**Ending C: Corrupted Power**
- One member absorbs Nyx's power
- Becomes corrupted but controlled
- Group must redeem corrupted member
- Dark story questline in post-game

**Ending D: Dark Victory** (requires specific choices)
- Malachar wins, becomes final boss
- Much harder fight, kingdom lost
- Can still win, complete different ending
- Bleak but completable

**Reward**: 
- 10000 XP (shared)
- Ending-specific legendary items
- New Game+ unlocked
- Post-game content access

## BAGIAN 2: SIDE QUESTS (15 Side Quests Tersebar di berbagai level)

### SIDE QUEST 1: "THE MISSING MERCHANT" (Level 2-4)
**Giver**: Riverside innkeeper  
**Location**: Riverside Village → Forest roads  
**Type**: Investigation + escort

**Narasi**:
Local merchant missing for 3 days. Innkeeper worried, asks untuk investigate. Turns out merchant kidnapped oleh bandits untuk ransom. Para pemain bisa negotiate, rescue, atau... make different choice.

**Objectives**:
1. Find merchant at bandit camp
2. Negotiate atau fight untuk release
3. Escort merchant back safely
4. Optional: Track bandit leader

**Reward**: 150 Gold, merchant's gratitude (future discounts), 400 XP

---

### SIDE QUEST 2: "THE PRIEST'S BLESSING" (Level 5-8)
**Giver**: Village priest  
**Location**: Riverside shrine, Dark forest  
**Type**: Dungeon + Puzzle + boss

**Narasi**:
Ancient shrine di hutan corrupted oleh dark magic. Priest meminta para pemain untuk purify shrine dan collect holy relics. Completed shrine provides healing benefits untuk dungeon runs.

**Objectives**:
1. Find 3 holy relics
2. Defeat corrupted guardian
3. Perform purification ritual
4. Return before sunset (time limit)

**Reward**: 300 Gold, holy water (consumables), priest's blessing (+10% XP), 700 XP

---

### SIDE QUEST 3: "HUNTER'S CHALLENGE" (Level 8-12)
**Giver**: Master Hunter  
**Location**: Kingdom hunting grounds  
**Type**: Hunting (multiple targets)

**Narasi**:
Master Hunter testing para pemain's hunting skills. Mereka harus hunt 4 rare beasts untuk prove capability. Each beast memiliki unique behavior dan location challenges.

**Objectives**:
1. Hunt Crimson Boar (forest)
2. Hunt Shadow Elk (grassland)
3. Hunt Cursed Bear (mountain)
4. Hunt Venomous Wolves (swamp)

**Reward**: 600 Gold, hunter's cloak, animal trophies, 1200 XP

---

### SIDE QUEST 4: "SCHOLAR'S COLLECTION" (Level 10-15)
**Giver**: Royal Scholar  
**Location**: Royal Archives → various dungeons  
**Type**: Collection + research

**Narasi**:
Scholar researching ancient history, needs para pemain untuk collect lost books dan artifacts. Scattered di various locations, some protected oleh monsters atau puzzles.

**Objectives**:
1. Find 5 ancient books (hidden locations)
2. Collect 3 artifact fragments
3. Solve library puzzle
4. Deliver ke scholar

**Reward**: 500 Gold, scholar's notes (increase XP gain by 5%), 1000 XP

---

### SIDE QUEST 5: "THE ALCHEMIST'S PROBLEM" (Level 12-18)
**Giver**: Alchemist NPC  
**Location**: Alchemist lab → ingredient gathering zones  
**Type**: Collection + escort

**Narasi**:
Alchemist needs rare ingredients untuk create powerful potions. Para pemain harus gather ingredients dari dangerous locations. Some ingredients guarded oleh rare monsters dengan special loot.

**Objectives**:
1. Gather moonflower (cave)
2. Collect dreamvine (forest)
3. Find crystal heart (mine)
4. Deliver safely

**Reward**: 400 Gold, alchemist potions (rare), 5% mana regen bonus, 900 XP

---

### SIDE QUEST 6: "BANDITS' REDEMPTION" (Level 15-20)
**Giver**: Reformed bandit NPC  
**Location**: Forest camp, kingdom outskirts  
**Type**: Recruitment + loyalty

**Narasi**:
Former bandit meminta help untuk reform bandit gang mereka. Para pemain must convince other bandits untuk stop crime dan join legitimate cause. Series of encounters dengan different bandit factions.

**Objectives**:
1. Meet dengan 3 bandit leaders
2. Convince atau defeat opposition
3. Establish legitimate trading post
4. Protect settlement dari bandits

**Reward**: 700 Gold, loyal allies (NPC support), 1500 XP

---

### SIDE QUEST 7: "THE NOBLE'S CURSE" (Level 18-24)

**Location**: Manor, cursed land  
**Type**: Curse removal + combat

**Narasi**:
Noble cursed oleh dark magic, cannot leave manor. Para pemain must find curse source dan remove it. Involves finding materials, defeating curse-bearing creature, dan cleansing ritual.

**Objectives**:
1. Research curse origin
2. Find 4 cleansing materials
3. Defeat curse keeper (Level 22, 900 HP)
4. Perform rituals di specific locations

**Reward**: 800 Gold, noble's mansion access (safe rest point), noble's family ring, 1800 XP

**Type**: Underwater exploration + recovery

Ancient shipwreck discovered near coast. Keeper asks para pemain untuk dive down, recover valuables, dan discover ship's history. Underwater creatures dan environmental hazards present.

**Objectives**:
1. Dive to wreck (manage oxygen)
2. Recover 5 valuable items
3. Find captain's log
4. Return safely

Grand arena tournament untuk test warriors. Para pemain dapat participate untuk earn glory, money, dan unique rewards. Series of fights against increasingly difficult opponents.

**Objectives**:
1. Win 5 arena matches (difficulty scaling)
